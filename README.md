### About

I am a **postdoc at the Pattern Recognition Group, University of Bern**, working on **graph and spatio-temporal machine learning**. The connecting thread across everything I do is **graph representations of structured data**, from theory (graph kernels, graph edit distances, the pre-image and generation problem) to applications across science and industry. I am open to **Machine Learning Research Scientist / Research Engineer** roles.

### What I work on

- **Graph machine learning**: graph kernels, graph edit distances, GNNs, and the pre-image / generation problem. Author of [`graphkit-learn`](https://github.com/jajupmochi/graphkit-learn) (129 stars; Pattern Recognition Letters, Expert Systems with Applications).
- **Spatio-temporal learning**: Transformer time encoders, GNNs, and per-station entity embeddings for Swiss river-temperature forecasting (SNSF). First-author paper accepted at **ICPR 2026**. Building [LIULIAN](https://github.com/LIULIAN-AI/liulian-python), an open-source spatio-temporal data platform with LLM agents.
- **Graph AI across domains**: computational chemistry (molecular property prediction, J. Comp. Chem. 2024), smart engineering (Innosuisse PLANALYSER, one of the principal contributors: ViT / YOLO / Segment Anything + graph reconstruction), digital humanities (handwriting recognition), and FinTech (production LLM-agent systems).

### Selected publications

- **Spatio-temporal Transformer with graph neural networks** for Swiss river-temperature forecasting. *ICPR 2026* (first author).
- **Predicting Redox Potentials by Graph-Based Machine Learning Methods.** *Journal of Computational Chemistry*, 2024 (first author).
- **Graph kernels, graph edit distances, and the graph pre-image problem** (the `graphkit-learn` line). *Pattern Recognition Letters*; *Expert Systems with Applications*.
- Full list on [Google Scholar](https://scholar.google.com/citations?user=cnlixw0AAAAJ&hl=en).
